Frequently Asked Question

Have questions about cats? Find the answer below!

Why do cats knead with their paws?

Cats knead with their paws as a comforting behavior that dates back to kittenhood when they kneaded their mothers to stimulate milk flow. Adult cats may continue kneading as a way to show affection or to mark their territory.

How can i tell if my cat is happy?

A happy cat will exhibit behaviors like purring, slow blinking, rubbing against you, and holding their tail upright. They may also play actively and seek out your company.

What foods are dangerous for cats?

Foods such as chocolate, onions, garlic, grapes, and raisins are toxic to cats. Additionally, avoid feeding them bones, raw meat, or anything seasoned with salt or spices.

How often should i take my cat to the vet?

It is recommended to take your cat to the vet at least once a year for a general check-up. For kittens and senior cats, more frequent visits may be necessary to monitor their health.
